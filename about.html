About YASINOVA

Complete Construction Ecosystem – From Vision to Victory

Yasinova is India's most comprehensive construction platform, connecting rural material suppliers with urban builders, engineers, architects, and interior designers—all powered by cutting-edge 3D design technology.

One Platform. Every Construction Need.

  • Materials: Direct sourcing of bricks, legal sand, steel, cement from verified rural suppliers
  • Professionals: Certified builders, civil engineers, architects, interior designers with verified credentials
  • 3D Design Studio: Interactive 3D house designs and virtual walkthroughs for every project
  • Smart Technology: BIM integration, AI material estimation, real-time project tracking

Why Yasinova Stands Out

  • End-to-End Solution – Design, materials, professionals, execution
  • 3D Visualization – See your home before building starts
  • Verified Network – Every professional is vetted and rated
  • Cost Precision – AI-powered material calculation from 3D models
  • Rural Empowerment – Village suppliers powering urban dreams
